Settings & Customization

Personalize Your AI Experience

Customize Know It All to match your preferences. Configure languages, explanation styles, summary types, and more to get the perfect AI-powered browsing experience.

Language Preferences

Configure your preferred languages for AI responses and translations.

Preferred Language

Set the default language for all AI responses. The extension will provide explanations, summaries, and other content in this language.

Examples:
  • English (default)
  • Spanish
  • French
  • German
  • Hindi
  • Any other language supported by Gemini AI

Translation Language

Choose the target language for the Translate feature. When you select text and click "Translate," it will be converted to this language.

Popular choices:
  • Spanish - Great for learning or travel
  • French - Business and cultural content
  • Mandarin Chinese - International communication
  • Japanese - Anime, manga, and tech content
  • Arabic - Middle Eastern content

Translation Mode

Choose between two translation modes to match your learning style.

Available modes:
  • Default Translate: Quick, straightforward translation of the selected text
  • Translate to Learn: Detailed word-by-word breakdown with pronunciation guides, grammar notes, and example usage - perfect for language learners

Explanation Types

Select how you want complex topics explained to match your learning preference.

Causal Explanations

Focuses on cause-and-effect relationships. Explains why something happens and what leads to specific outcomes.

Best for: Understanding scientific processes, historical events, or logical reasoning

Sequential Explanations

Breaks down information into step-by-step processes. Explains how something works in chronological order.

Best for: Learning procedures, algorithms, recipes, or any process-oriented content

Descriptive Explanations

Provides detailed descriptions of characteristics, features, and properties. Explains what something is.

Best for: Understanding concepts, objects, or phenomena through detailed descriptions

Comparative Explanations

Explains by comparing and contrasting with similar concepts. Highlights similarities and differences.

Best for: Understanding new concepts by relating them to familiar ones

Analytical Explanations

Breaks down complex topics into components and examines relationships between parts.

Best for: Deep understanding of systems, structures, or complex theories

Interpretive Explanations

Provides meaning and context. Explains significance, implications, and deeper understanding.

Best for: Literature, philosophy, art, or content requiring contextual understanding

Summary Types

Choose how you want lengthy content summarized to match your reading goals.

Descriptive Summary

Objectively describes the main points without interpretation or evaluation.

Best for: News articles, factual content, or when you need unbiased information

Evaluative Summary

Includes critical assessment and judgment of the content's quality and validity.

Best for: Research papers, opinion pieces, or when you need quality assessment

Narrative Summary

Tells the story in a flowing, chronological manner with emphasis on key events.

Best for: Stories, case studies, historical accounts, or sequential content

Informative Summary

Focuses on conveying key facts and information in a clear, concise manner.

Best for: Educational content, tutorials, or informational articles

Thematic Summary

Organizes content around central themes and ideas rather than chronological order.

Best for: Complex articles with multiple topics or thematic analysis

Executive Summary

Brief, high-level overview focusing on key findings, conclusions, and recommendations.

Best for: Business reports, research papers, or when you need quick insights

Critical Summary

Analyzes strengths, weaknesses, and implications of the content.

Best for: Academic papers, arguments, or content requiring critical thinking

Synoptic Summary (Synthesis)

Combines information from multiple sources or sections into a unified overview.

Best for: Long documents, multiple perspectives, or comprehensive understanding

Précis

Ultra-concise summary maintaining the original tone and structure in minimal words.

Best for: When you need the absolute shortest summary possible

Programming Language Preference

Set your preferred programming language for the Code Generator feature.

Preferred Programming Language

Choose which programming language the AI should use when generating code solutions. Select "Auto-detect" to let the AI choose the best language for each problem.

Supported languages:
  • Auto-detect: AI chooses the best language for the task
  • Python: Great for data science, scripting, and general purpose
  • JavaScript: Web development and frontend applications
  • TypeScript: Type-safe JavaScript for larger projects
  • Java: Enterprise applications and Android development
  • C++: Performance-critical applications and systems programming
  • C#: .NET applications and game development (Unity)
  • Go: Cloud services and backend systems
  • Rust: Systems programming with memory safety
  • Ruby: Web development with Rails
  • PHP: Server-side web development
  • Swift: iOS and macOS applications
  • Kotlin: Modern Android development

Tips for Best Results

Get the most out of Know It All with these customization tips.

Match Your Learning Style

Choose explanation types that align with how you learn best. Visual learners may prefer descriptive, while logical thinkers might prefer causal.

Experiment with Settings

Try different summary types and explanation styles to find what works best for different types of content.

Use Learn Mode

Enable "Translate to Learn" mode when studying a new language to get detailed breakdowns and pronunciation guides.

Save Your Preferences

Your settings are saved automatically and synced across all your devices where you use Chrome.
